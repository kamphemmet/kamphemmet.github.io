My name is Helena Gomér.

I would have liked to reflect more on the work and life on the farm but there is unfortunately no such documentation, one can only speculate. It was not always so easy to get hold of the facts and analyze what you come up with, but I have learned to draw inferences and the result is a description from my point of view. The work has gone well and it's been a lot of fun. I've learned a lot about my home I did not know before, but also why it looks like it does today.

I want to thank Dan Gomér who made it possible for me to convert my original school work to a website.

If you have any comments concerning the contents of this site, I would be very grateful if you forward them to me. Maybe you have more information that I can add?

Stockholm, Januari, 2005
