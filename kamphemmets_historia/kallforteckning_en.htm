List of sources

My main information I've got from interviews with my parents and my grandparents but I have also visited the Regional Archives in Vadstena to find out who lived on the farm and also visited land survey in Linköping where there are old maps of the area. I have also had to borrow maps from an acquaintance named Stig öberg, Väderstad, a man with a solid interest of one of the other farms' past, namely the neighboring farm Berns.
