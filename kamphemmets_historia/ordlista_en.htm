Glossary

Nobility - freedom. From the late 1200s, meaning exempt from tax in return for campaign war service to hurry, as of 1569 the same as the nobility.

Manor - the main courtyard and generally ordinary goods, in an aristocratic family's goods holdings. It was exempt from taxes and other charges (seat of freedom).

Homestead - called to the difference from the apartment such land ownership in the country, which was set in manpower due to its ability to only certain taxes.

Rå and rörs homestead - own independent farm with its own limits not included in any village.
